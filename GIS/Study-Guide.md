## ğŸ’¡ Top Exam Tips for GIS

1.  **Visual Explanations:** GIS is a visual subject. Always draw diagrams for concepts like Buffer, Overlay, Vector vs Raster, and System Architecture.
2.  **Differentiations:** Common questions ask to differentiate:
    - Raster vs Vector
    - Spatial vs Attribute Data
    - Verification vs Validation
    - Accuracy vs Precision
3.  **Technical Terms:** Be precise with terms like "Topology", "Georeferencing", "Map Projection", and "Metadata".
4.  **Spatial Analysis:** Understand the logic behind local, focal, zonal, and global operations.
5.  **Modern Trends:** Don't ignore Unit 7 (Open GIS) and Unit 6 (SDI). These are increasingly important.
